Who Am I?

I'm Mavis and stuff, the 24 year old, runaway American, who majored in Design Innovation. I became interested in learning about web design because I love coded art and am fascinated by the tech art scene. Studying web design in and out of university has given me a new medium to explore these interests through and I'm excited to expand my knowledge even further! This website is partially a portfolio but has predominantly been a tool to teach myself more about coding in html and css so I can see what is possible in the medium. I was in an indie rock band, The Snaz, for 5 years in which I acted as keyboardist and manager. That doesn't have much to do with web design, but it did give me experience planning events, designing merch and promotional material, and organizing other people.

Bartending and Hospitality
