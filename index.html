Who Am I?

I'm Mavis and stuff, 24 years old, runaway American, who majored in Design Innovation. I became interested in learning about Web Design because I love coded art and am fascinated by the tech art scene. Studying Web Design in and out of University has given me a new medium to explore these interests through and I'm excited to expand my knowledge even further! This website is partially a portfolio but has predominantly been a tool to teach myself more about coding in html and css so I can see what is possible in the medium. If you want to see an interactive example of the kind of creative coding projects I like to create you can click on the purple sidebar to the left and if you would like to see a portfolio of my visual art you can click on the red box below.

I have been working as a bartender, specifically in craft beer breweries and bars, for the last 4 years. I have a lot of experience working on a team and I know way too much about beer. I also have a lot of experience dealing with a wide range of people and solving problems as they arise.
